## The System: 4 Revenue Streams

**Revenue: $1,200/month**

I created 70 video scripts using AI. Not generic — scripts about building with AI, using AI, thoughts on AI.

Then I used Blotato (automation tool) to post 1-2 shorts per day automatically.

No filming me. Just scripts → AI video generation → Blotato → auto-upload to YouTube.

Result: 2,000+ subscribers, 50K+ views, YouTube Super Chat revenue.

**Time invested:** 20 hours (script creation + setup)
**Monthly revenue:** $1,200
**Hourly rate:** $60/hour (and still growing)

### 2. Email List & Products ($1,800/month)
**Revenue: $1,800/month**

From YouTube, I drove people to a landing page. They entered their email to get my "AI Lifestyle OS" guide.

500 people on email list.

Then I created 3 digital products:
- AI Lifestyle OS: $143/month (course + community)
- UGC Creator Playbook: $97 (one-time)
- Viral Content Blueprint: $197 (one-time)

Conversion rate: 15% (50 people → 7-8 customers)

**Revenue:** $1,800/month
**Time invested:** 40 hours (product creation)
**Ongoing effort:** 2 hours/week (community management)

### 3. Affiliate & Partnerships ($600/month)
**Revenue: $600/month**

I recommended tools I actually use (Blotato, AI tools, hosting). 

Some are affiliate links (earn commission). Some are partnerships.

Passive revenue just for mentioning tools I'd recommend anyway.
