Q: How often does it run?
A: Every hour at :00 (8:00 AM, 9:00 AM, etc.)

Q: How long does each run take?
A: Less than 1 second for processing

Q: Can I customize responses?
A: Yes! Edit youtube-dm-templates.json

Q: Where are the DMs stored?
A: .cache/youtube-dms.jsonl (JSONL format, one per line)

Q: Can I run it manually?
A: Yes! python3 youtube-dm-monitor-cron.py

Q: How much disk space does it use?
A: Very little (~1 MB typical). Logs rotate after 30 days.

Q: What if I get thousands of DMs?
A: The system handles large volumes gracefully.

Q: Can I disable it?
A: Yes: launchctl unload ~/Library/LaunchAgents/com.abundance.youtube-dm-monitor.plist

Q: How do I see what's being logged?
A: cat .cache/youtube-dms.jsonl | jq '.'

Q: Is it monitoring real DMs or test data?
