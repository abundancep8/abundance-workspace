## POSTING SCHEDULE - OPTIMAL TIMES

- **Frequency:** More frequent = better (algorithm favors active accounts)
- **Consistency:** Same time daily = better (audience expects it)
- **Spacing:** 4-6 hours apart = optimal (avoids internal suppression)
- **Time of day:** 
  - 6-8 AM PDT (morning viewers)
  - 12-1 PM PDT (lunch break)
