# Quick-Start Templates & Copy Paste Resources

## 1. LinkedIn Connection Request Templates (Ready to Use)

### Template A: Medical Practice Owner (Personalized)
```
Hi [NAME],

Just came across [PRACTICE NAME]'s growth into [NEW SERVICE/LOCATION].

Your approach to [SPECIFIC ASPECT] seems different from most practices 
in the area - curious if you're also tackling [PAIN POINT] as you scale?

No pitch here, just genuinely interested in how you're doing it.

[YOUR NAME]
```

**How to customize in 2 minutes:**
- Replace [PRACTICE NAME] with actual practice name
- Check their LinkedIn "About" for recent moves or focuses
- Pick pain point relevant to their size/specialty

---

### Template B: Practice Manager (Semi-Personalized)
```
Hi [NAME],

I work with practices in [AREA] on [SPECIFIC OUTCOME: improving patient 
flow / reducing no-shows / streamlining compliance].

We just helped a 6-doctor group similar to yours see [SPECIFIC RESULT], 
and I wondered if [PAIN POINT] is on your radar at all?

No pressure - just thought it was worth flagging.

[YOUR NAME]
```

**Customization tips:**
- Replace [AREA] with actual metro area
- Research the practice they work at, estimate size
- Pick result that matches their likely revenue level

---

## 3. LinkedIn Article Outline Templates

### Article 1: Pain Point Deep Dive
```
Title: "[Practice Type] Are Losing $[AMOUNT] Annually to [PAIN POINT]. Here's How."

Outline:
- Hook: "I analyzed [X] practices and found..."
- Problem statement: 3 specific sub-problems within main pain point
- Why it matters: Financial impact + emotional/operational impact
- Case study: Real example (anonymized)
- Solution framework: 3-5 steps they can implement
- Metrics: What changes should they expect?
- CTA: Comment with your biggest challenge on this topic

Word count: 800-1200 words
```

---

### Article 2: How-To / Framework
```
Title: "[X]-Step Framework [Practice Type] Owners Use to [OUTCOME]"

Outline:
- Introduction: Why most practices struggle
- Step 1: Detailed explanation + example
- Step 2: Detailed explanation + example
- Step 3: Detailed explanation + example
- Common mistakes: What NOT to do
- How to measure success: Specific KPIs
- CTA: Share your experience in comments

Word count: 800-1200 words
```

---

### Article 3: Industry Trend/Data
```
Title: "[TREND] is Changing How [Practice Type] Practices Operate (Data)"

Outline:
- Headline finding: "We looked at X practices and found..."

- What it means: Implications for owners/managers
```

## 5. Objection Response Scripts

### "We're happy with our current solution"
```
I totally get that - [SOLUTION] is solid for [THEIR USE CASE].

Where we typically add value is [SPECIFIC CAPABILITY DIFFERENT FROM THEIRS].

Most practices don't realize they're leaving [SPECIFIC REVENUE/TIME] on 
the table because their current solution doesn't do [SPECIFIC THING].

But if you're genuinely happy, no need to change. Just wanted to flag it.
```

---

### "We don't have budget"
```
I hear that a lot. Here's what I'd ask:

If we could show you recover your investment in [TIMEFRAME] through 
[SPECIFIC SAVING], would that change the conversation?

Because that's what we typically see. No obligation to pursue it, 
but worth understanding the potential, right?
```

---

### "This isn't a priority right now"
```
Fair - you've got more urgent things.

Quick question though: when does [PAIN POINT] become a priority? 
[NEXT QUARTER / DURING HIRING SEASON / AFTER BUSY SEASON]?

Asking because the practices that tackle it early tend to get way 
better results than those who wait until it's urgent.

But I get if timing's not right. Whenever you want to chat, I'm here.
```

---

### "Can you send me information?"
```
Happy to.

Quick question first - what would actually be useful?

A) Case study from a similar practice
B) 2-minute video demo
C) ROI calculation for your specific situation

That way I'm not sending generic stuff you'll never read.

Which of those three would actually help you make a decision?
```

## 8. Call Agenda Template

```
CALL AGENDA: [PROSPECT NAME] - 15 minutes

[0-1 min] Intro & thank them for time
[1-3 min] Context: "I help practices like yours with [OUTCOME]"
[3-7 min] Discovery questions:
  - "What's your biggest challenge with [PAIN POINT] right now?"
  - "How is this impacting the practice? (Revenue, operations, team morale)"
  - "Have you tried solving this before? What happened?"
  - "What would success look like for you?"

[7-10 min] Share relevant insight/case study
  - "Other practices in your situation have seen [OUTCOME]"
  - Show 1-2 specific metrics from case study

[10-13 min] Identify fit
  - "Does this sound like something worth exploring?"
  - "If yes, next step would be [SPECIFIC NEXT STEP]"
  - If no: "Totally fair - if things change, keep me in mind"

[13-15 min] Logistics
  - Confirm next step
  - Send calendar link
  - Thank them again

NOTES:
- Listen 70%, talk 30%
- Don't pitch product - diagnose problem
- End with "fit assessment," not "close"
```

## Usage Instructions

1. **Read through all templates** - understand the structure
2. **Pick 2-3 that match your style** - don't feel obligated to use all
3. **Customize with real details** - removes [BRACKETS], add specific practices/metrics
4. **Test and measure** - track response rates for each approach
5. **Refine based on what works** - update templates with winners
