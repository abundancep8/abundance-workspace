# 90-Day Multi-Vertical Campaign Plan

**Objective:** Launch coordinated outreach across all three tiers simultaneously, testing messaging and generating qualified pipeline for 90 days.

**Success Metrics:**
- 100+ outreach activities (LinkedIn, email, calls) per week
- 30-40% response rate on initial outreach
- 8-10% conversion from response → qualified demo
- 2-3 closed deals per tier per month (starting month 2)

---

## Campaign Architecture

### Phase 1: Weeks 1-4 (Foundation & Launch)
**Goal:** Build messaging, identify target lists, launch first touch

### Phase 2: Weeks 5-8 (Nurturing & Qualification)
**Goal:** Follow-up, schedule demos, build pipeline

### Phase 3: Weeks 9-12 (Closing & Expansion)
**Goal:** Close deals, collect testimonials, plan upsells

---

## TIER 1: Medical & Dental Practices (High-Value Campaign)

### Target Audience

**Primary Targets:**
- Practice owners / Founders (decision makers)
- Practice managers (influencers)
- Title keywords: "Owner," "Practice Manager," "Managing Director," "CEO"
- Company size: 3-50 employees
- LinkedIn behavior: Active, responsive to medical content

**Geographic focus (Priority Order):**
1. United States (highest concentration)
2. Canada, UK (English-speaking, strong healthcare markets)
3. Australia, New Zealand
4. Western Europe (Germany, France, Nordic countries)

**List size target:** 2,000-3,000 qualified prospects

---

### Phase 1: Foundation (Weeks 1-4)

#### Week 1-2: Research & List Building

**Activities:**
1. Build list of 500 medical practices (US Northeast region)
   - Use LinkedIn Sales Navigator (criteria: medical practice, 5-50 employees)
   - Cross-reference with Dun & Bradstreet for validation
   - Flag high-engagement practices (active job postings, recent content)

2. Build list of 500 dental practices
   - Same methodology
   - Flag specialty practices (dermatology, orthodontics) separately

3. Build list of 200 legal practices
   - Solo + small firm focus
   - Areas: Corporate law, IP, tax, family law (highest CRM adoption potential)

**Messaging Framework (Created):**
- **Subject line:** "[Practice Name] - $48K annual waste in no-shows (we found it)"
- **Angle:** Calculated no-show loss + easy fix
- **Proof:** Industry benchmarks + case study

#### Week 2-3: Creative Development

**Assets to Create:**
1. **Email sequence #1 (5 emails, 10-day cadence):**
   - Email 1: Problem recognition ("Your no-shows cost $2,000-$5,000/month")
   - Email 2: Social proof (case study from similar practice)
   - Email 3: Differentiation (vs. their current system)
   - Email 4: FOMO (limited free trial spots)
   - Email 5: Final close (low-commitment offer)

2. **LinkedIn messaging sequence:**
   - Connection request: "Saw your practice grows 20% YoY. Impressive."
   - Follow-up (3 days): "Quick thought on clinic scheduling..."
   - Follow-up (7 days): "Want to see how [Similar Practice] cut no-shows 40%?"

3. **Case study video (3 min):**
   - Dental practice owner talking about pain → solution → results
   - Metrics: "60% no-show rate → 25% in 4 weeks + $48K annual recovery"

4. **ROI calculator (web tool):**
   - Input: # of appointments, current no-show %, avg fee
   - Output: Annual waste + potential recovery with 30% reduction

#### Week 3-4: Launch

**Week 3 Activities:**
- Load lists into CRM (HubSpot, Salesforce, or Pipedrive)
- Set up email domain reputation (warmup domain if new)
- Launch LinkedIn outreach: 30-50 connection requests/day (Tier 1 target)
- Monitor initial response rates (target: 20-25% for medical/dental)

**Week 4 Activities:**
- Begin email sequence with initial responders
- Update LinkedIn messaging with warm respondents
- Monitor engagement metrics (open rate, reply rate, positive/negative response)
- Adjust messaging based on feedback

**Metrics at end of Week 4:**
- LinkedIn connections: 200-300 accepted
- Email opens: 35-45% (good for cold)
- Email replies: 5-8% (very good for cold outreach)
- Qualified conversations: 10-15

---

### Phase 2: Nurturing (Weeks 5-8)

#### Week 5: Qualification & Demo Booking

**Activities:**
1. **Respond to all initial replies** with:
   - Personalized 2-min video (shows I'm real, not bot)
   - Free ROI calculation (customized to their practice size)
   - 15-min discovery call invite

2. **Cold-call warm responders** (not the "not interested" — the "maybes")
   - Script: "Hey [Name], I sent you info on reducing no-shows. Are you open to a quick call?"
   - Goal: 5-minute conversation to gauge interest

3. **Launch Phase 2 email sequence** (nurture for non-responders):
   - Different angle: "One thing we noticed about practices like yours..."
   - Social proof: "8 practices in your state are using us"
   - Case study: "#2 in this sequence goes deeper on results"

#### Week 6: Demo Scheduling

**Activities:**
1. **Schedule demos** with qualified prospects:
   - Goal: 10-15 demos by end of week
   - Demo length: 30 minutes (not 60 — respects their time)
   - Demo structure:
     - 5 min: Problem diagnosis ("You mentioned no-shows are ~20%")
     - 15 min: Solution walkthrough (specific to their pain)
     - 10 min: Q&A + next steps

2. **Send pre-demo assets:**
   - ROI calculator result
   - Case study (tailored vertical)
   - Free trial offer (14 days, no credit card)

3. **LinkedIn engagement:** Like/comment on prospects' recent posts (stay visible)

#### Week 7-8: Demo Execution & Pipeline Building

**Week 7:**
- Execute 10-15 demos
- Track outcomes: Interested → Free trial OR Not interested → Nurture
- Document pain points / objections (feed back to messaging team)

**Week 8:**
- Follow up with "Not ready yet" prospects (nurture track)
- Prepare proposals for "Interested" prospects
- Collect case study opportunities from early trial adopters

**Metrics at end of Phase 2:**
- Demos conducted: 30-40
- Free trial starts: 15-25
- Proposals sent: 5-8

---

### Phase 3: Closing (Weeks 9-12)

#### Week 9: Trial Support & Expansion Outreach

**Activities:**
1. **Support trial users:**
   - Daily check-in (Day 1-3): Training, onboarding
   - Mid-trial (Day 7): "How's it going? Any questions?"
   - Pre-end (Day 13): "Before trial ends, here's what we can offer..."

2. **Expand initial demo list:**
   - 100 new prospect list load
   - New messaging angle: "Practices are cutting no-shows 40%. Here's why..."
   - Goal: 20-30 new demos scheduled by end of week

3. **Nurture proposals:**
   - Check in with proposal recipients: "Any questions about the plan?"
   - Address hesitations (usually budget or implementation concern)
   - Offer 90-day ROI guarantee

#### Week 10: Closing Push

**Activities:**
1. **Close early trial converters:**
   - "You've seen the results. Ready to go live?"
   - Offer: Annual discount (20% off) for commitment
   - Timeline: Go-live in next 5 business days

2. **Secondary demos** (those who didn't respond initially):
   - "I know you're busy. 15-min call this week?" (lower commitment)
   - Angle: "Let's just talk about your biggest scheduling pain"

3. **Collect success stories:**
   - From closed deals: "Would you do a quick 3-min video testimonial?"
   - From trial users: Document early results (no-show reduction %)

#### Week 11-12: Month-End Execution

**Activities:**
1. **Final closing push:**
   - All proposals outstanding: "Let's close this out"
   - Offer final incentives (annual commitment discount, free add-ons)

2. **Testimonial & case study creation:**
   - Video interviews with new customers
   - Document metrics: no-show reduction, revenue impact, time saved
   - Publish 1-2 case studies by end of week

3. **Plan for Month 2 expansion:**
   - Identify which messaging worked best
   - Scale to 500+ new prospects
   - Identify which verticals (dental vs. medical) converted better

**Metrics at end of Phase 3:**
- Deals closed: 5-8 per tier
- Trial to close rate: 40-50%
- CLTV per closed deal: $15K-$25K
- Case studies captured: 2-3

## TIER 2: High-Volume Services (Salons, Fitness, Restaurants)

### Target Audience

**Primary Targets:**
- Hair salon owners
- Fitness studio owners
- Independent restaurant owners
- Nail salon managers/owners
- Tattoo shop owners

**List size target:** 3,000-5,000 qualified prospects per vertical (9,000-15,000 total)

---

### Campaign Structure (Adapted for Faster Sales Cycle)

**Key Differences from Tier 1:**
- Shorter email sequences (3-email instead of 5-email)
- Faster demo scheduling (offer free trial immediately)
- More visual/content-driven (Instagram, TikTok links in outreach)
- Lower-touch nurturing (higher volume = less hand-holding)

#### Phase 1: Foundation (Weeks 1-3, Compressed)

**Week 1:**
1. Build targeted lists:
   - 1,000 hair salons (US major cities)
   - 800 fitness studios
   - 800 independent restaurants
   - 400 nail salons

2. Research & content creation:
   - "Salon owner checklist: 5 ways you're losing customers" (downloadable)
   - "Fitness studio: cut no-show rate in half" (case study)
   - "Restaurant bookings: from chaotic to streamlined" (explainer video, 2 min)

**Week 2:**
1. Launch Phase 1 outreach:
   - LinkedIn connection: 100-150/day (Tier 2 faster responses)
   - Email: 50-100/day from cold list
   - Messaging angle: "Your biggest bottleneck (we know what it is)"

**Week 3:**
1. Monitor response rates
2. Begin qualified discovery calls (no formal demos yet — shorter calls)
3. Offer free trial immediately (no discovery call required)

#### Phase 2: Nurturing (Weeks 4-7)

**Week 4-5:**
1. High-volume email nurturing (batch sends):
   - "Can't book an appointment anywhere" (salon pain)
   - "Your gym's no-show rate is costing you" (fitness pain)
   - "Restaurant table management chaos" (F&B pain)

2. Free trial signups tracking:
   - Expect 100-200 trial starts across verticals
   - Auto-email onboarding sequence

**Week 6-7:**
1. Trial user follow-up (lightweight):
   - Day 3: "How's your first week?"
   - Day 10: "Ready to go live?"
   - Convert to paid

#### Phase 3: Closing (Weeks 8-12)

**Week 8-9:**
1. Close early trial converters (high conversion rate expected: 50%+)
2. Expand initial list: 2,000 new prospects per vertical
3. Collect testimonials from closed deals (quick videos, 1-2 min)

**Week 10-12:**
1. Continue expansion to new prospect lists
2. Launch vertical-specific campaigns (e.g., "Salon owners in California")
3. Testimonial-driven outreach (leverage new case studies)

**Metrics at end of 12 weeks (Tier 2):**
- Deals closed: 15-20 across all verticals
- Trial to close rate: 50-60%
- CLTV per deal: $5K-$8K
- Testimonials: 5-10 video case studies
- Monthly recurring revenue: $3K-$5K

## TIER 3: Field Service (HVAC, Plumbing, Electrical, Contractors)

### Target Audience

**Primary Targets:**
- HVAC company owners
- Plumbing contractors
- Electrical contractors
- General contractors
- Auto repair shop owners
- Landscaping company owners

**List size target:** 3,000-5,000 qualified prospects per vertical (18,000+ total)

---

### Campaign Structure (Consultative, Longer Sales Cycle)

**Key Differences from Tier 1 & 2:**
- Longer nurturing cycle (60-90 days typical)
- More technical demo (routing algorithms, integrations)
- Owner + operations manager both influenced (multiple touches)
- Field-specific proof needed (show technician engagement metrics)

#### Phase 1: Foundation (Weeks 1-4)

**Week 1-2:**
1. Build targeted lists:
   - 1,200 HVAC contractors
   - 1,000 plumbers
   - 1,200 electricians
   - 500 general contractors
   - 600 auto repair shops
   - 800 landscapers

2. Create assets:
   - "Field service ROI calculator" (route optimization value + dispatch efficiency)
   - "Routing optimization explainer" (animated video, 3 min)
   - "Integration roadmap" (show which suppliers we work with)
   - Case study: HVAC company (clear metrics: drive time reduction, jobs completed)

**Week 3:**
1. Launch Phase 1 outreach:
   - LinkedIn: 75-100 connection requests/day (Tier 3 slower but more qualified)
   - Email: 50-75/day (lower volume, more personalized)
   - Angle: "We found $15K/year waste in your routing. Here's how."

**Week 4:**
1. Monitor engagement
2. Begin discovery conversations with warm respondents
3. Schedule working sessions (not demos yet — consultative first)

#### Phase 2: Nurturing & Diagnosis (Weeks 5-8)

**Week 5-6:**
1. Conduct working sessions (1-hour discovery):
   - Map current process
   - Identify specific pain points
   - Calculate financial impact
   - Show customized solution

2. Follow-up nurturing (for non-responders):
   - "Route optimization could save your company 10+ hours/week"
   - "Technician adoption in field service: here's how we got 95% adoption"
   - Case studies: Vertical-specific (HVAC ROI, plumbing no-show reduction, etc.)

**Week 7-8:**
1. Schedule formal demos (with working session insights)
2. Build custom ROI proposals (specific to their company size/fleet)
3. Prepare trial access

#### Phase 3: Closing (Weeks 9-12)

**Week 9-10:**
1. Launch formal trials (after working session + demo)
   - Load customer data
   - Invite 2-3 field technicians to test
   - 14-day trial period

2. Expand initial prospecting:
   - 500+ new prospects per vertical
   - Angle: "We're working with X number of contractors in your area. Here's what they found..."

3. Trial support:
   - Day 5 check-in: "Any wins so far?"
   - Day 10: "Here's what we're seeing from other contractors..."
   - Day 13: "Before trial ends, let's talk next steps"

**Week 11-12:**
1. Close trial users (target: 40-50% conversion)
2. Expand to additional locations / service areas
3. Collect testimonials & case study videos
4. Plan Month 2 scaling

**Metrics at end of 12 weeks (Tier 3):**
- Deals closed: 8-12
- Trial to close rate: 40-50%
- CLTV per deal: $8K-$15K
- Testimonials: 3-5 video case studies
- Monthly recurring revenue: $2K-$4K

## Cross-Tier Integration & Referral Strategy

### Tier-to-Tier Referrals

**Tier 1 → Tier 2:**
- Medical practices recommend to client-facing salons (networking)
- Dental practices recommend to wellness spas (complementary services)
- Legal practices recommend to boutique fitness studios (referral partnerships)

**Tier 2 → Tier 3:**
- Restaurants recommend to local cleaning services, HVAC (service provider networks)
- Salons recommend to local contractors / plumbers / electricians (referral partnerships)

**Tier 3 → Tier 1 & 2:**
- Contractors network with local medical practices (shared spaces)
- HVAC companies refer to office-based Tier 1/2 businesses

**Mechanics:**
1. Build referral network document (who refers to whom)
2. Create referral incentive (10-15% discount on annual renewal for successful referral)
3. Equip customers with referral links (easy sharing)

## Key Success Factors

1. **Consistency:** 100+ outreach activities/week for 12 weeks (non-negotiable)
2. **Iteration:** Track what works; double down on high-response messaging
3. **Personalization:** Hand-crafted follow-ups to warm leads (not mass automation)
4. **Speed:** Demo within 3 days of qualified conversation
5. **Proof:** Real case studies + testimonials by week 6 (reinforces credibility)
6. **Urgency:** Limited trial spots, annual discount deadlines (drives closing)
7. **Team:** Need dedicated outreach person + SDR function (can't do alone at scale)
