# LinkedIn Targeting Strategy for Medical Practices

## 1. Core Target Segments

### Segment A: Practice Owners & Physicians
**Primary Decision Makers**
- Title keywords: Owner, Founder, MD, DO, DDS, Principal, Managing Partner
- Seniority: C-level and Director level
- Decision authority: 95% (sole or final approval)
- Engagement style: Data-driven, ROI-focused
- Response time: Slower (busy), but high-value

**LinkedIn Targeting:**
```
Titles: "Medical Practice Owner" OR "Physician" OR "Practice Owner" OR "MD" OR "Dentist" OR "Chiropractor"
Industry: Healthcare Services, Medical Practices, Dental Offices
Location: Tier 1-2 US metro areas (higher density practices)
Company Size: 10-250 employees
```

### Segment B: Practice Managers & Office Managers
**Gatekeepers & Operational Decision Makers**
- Titles: Practice Manager, Office Manager, Operations Manager, Practice Administrator
- Seniority: Mid-level (Manager/Senior)
- Decision authority: 70% (recommend, owner approves)
- Engagement style: Process-focused, efficiency-driven
- Response time: Faster, more email-responsive

**LinkedIn Targeting:**
```
Titles: "Practice Manager" OR "Office Manager" OR "Operations Manager" OR "Administrator"
Industry: Healthcare Services, Medical Practices
Location: All US regions
Company Size: 10-250 employees
```

### Segment C: Clinical Operations & Compliance Roles
**Secondary Influencers**
- Titles: Clinical Director, Compliance Officer, Quality Manager, Lead Clinician
- Seniority: Mid-level
- Decision authority: 50% (influence through practice manager/owner)
- Engagement style: Specialty-focused, detail-oriented

## 3. Geographic & Demographic Targeting

### Priority Tier 1 (Highest Density)
- **Metropolitan areas:** Los Angeles, New York, Chicago, Houston, Phoenix, Philadelphia, San Antonio, San Diego, Dallas, San Jose
- **Rationale:** Higher practice density, more multi-location groups, bigger budgets
- **Expected Response Rate:** 12-18%

### Priority Tier 2 (Strong Secondary)
- **Suburban metros:** Denver, Atlanta, Seattle, Boston, Miami, Minneapolis, Austin, Portland
- **Rationale:** Growth market practices, less saturated with vendors
- **Expected Response Rate:** 10-15%

### Priority Tier 3 (Lower Volume, High Intent)
- **High-income areas:** Park Cities (Dallas), Orange County, Westchester (NY), Palo Alto
- **Rationale:** Premium practices with bigger budgets, less price-sensitive
- **Expected Response Rate:** 8-12%

## 4. Company Size & Revenue Targeting

| Size | Typical Pain Points | Annual Tech Budget | Response Rate |
|------|---------------------|-------------------|---------------|
| Solo (1-2 docs) | Staffing, compliance | $5-15K | 6-10% |
| Small group (3-5) | Revenue, efficiency | $15-35K | 10-14% |
| **Medium group (6-20)** ⭐ | Systems, growth | $35-100K | 14-20% |
| Large group (20+) | Integration, scale | $100K+ | 8-12% |

**Recommendation:** Focus initial campaigns on small-to-medium groups (3-20 doctors). They have:
- Real budget constraints (not just exploring)
- Faster decision cycles than solo practices
- More budget than solo, less bureaucracy than large groups

## 5. Audience Building Techniques

### LinkedIn Search Alerts (Organic)
1. Save 3-5 Boolean searches with filters
2. Check weekly for new prospects
3. Lowest cost, slowest growth (~20/week)

### LinkedIn Ads (Account-Based Marketing)
1. Create Custom Audience: Upload email list of practices
2. Add Lookalike Audience from existing customers
3. Target by job title + industry + location
4. Budget: $500-1,500/month for Tier 1 locations
5. Expected reach: 50-100 warm-qualified prospects/month

### Intent Signals (LinkedIn Data)
- Profile view: Visited your company page
- Skill endorsements: Recently endorsed for practice management
- Content engagement: Liked/commented on healthcare posts
- Job changes: Recently promoted to manager role
- Company news: Practice added new locations/funding

### List Building Strategy
1. **Manual outreach segment:** 50 prospects (Tier 1 metros, medium groups)
2. **Warm intro segment:** 100 prospects via referrals/mutual connections
3. **InMail segment:** 200 prospects (high-quality, low-touch)
4. **Content engagement segment:** 300+ prospects (attract via thought leadership)

## 6. Competitive Landscape on LinkedIn

### Who's Already Targeting Them
- **EMR/Practice Management Software:** NextGen, Athena, Kareo, Zocdoc
  - *Strategy:* Educational, feature-focused, product webinars
  - *Weakness:* Generic messaging, not vertical-specific

- **Healthcare Staffing:** Matchwell, Apex, RippleMatch
  - *Strategy:* Pain point (staffing shortages), quick ROI
  - *Weakness:* Transactional, no relationship building

- **Financial Services:** Dedicated medical lenders, practice accounting firms
  - *Strategy:* Revenue optimization, tax planning
  - *Weakness:* Not consultative, assumes financial problems

- **Compliance/Regulatory:** HIPAA consultants, compliance firms
  - *Strategy:* Risk-avoidance angle, fear-based
  - *Weakness:* Negative tone, not growth-focused

### Your Competitive Advantage
If you're selling **practice improvement, efficiency, or revenue growth:**
- **Differentiate:** Show specific practice revenue lifts (case studies)
- **Own the category:** Be THE expert in your vertical (not a generic consultant)
- **Build authority:** LinkedIn articles, webinars, industry metrics
- **Warm-first model:** Prioritize warm intros over cold outreach

## 7. Account-Based Marketing (ABM) Framework

### Tier 1 Accounts (Close Rate: 25-35%)
- **Criteria:** 10-20 doctors, $500K+ annual revenue potential
- **Approach:** Personalized, multi-touch (email + LinkedIn + phone)
- **Investment:** 2-3 hours per account research
- **Cadence:** 7-9 touchpoints over 30 days

### Tier 2 Accounts (Close Rate: 15-25%)
- **Criteria:** 5-10 doctors, $100-300K potential
- **Approach:** Targeted messaging + case studies
- **Investment:** 30 minutes per account
- **Cadence:** 5-7 touchpoints over 45 days

### Tier 3 Accounts (Close Rate: 8-15%)
- **Criteria:** Solo-3 doctors, $25-100K potential
- **Approach:** Template messaging + scalable outreach
- **Investment:** 5-10 minutes per account
- **Cadence:** 3-5 touchpoints over 60 days
