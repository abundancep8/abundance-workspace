Copy-Paste and Customize for Your Clients

Tone: Authentic, relatable, conversational (not salesy)

(Works for SaaS, apps, tools)

It's draining, it's repetitive, and honestly? I'd rather be doing literally anything else.

Then I found [PRODUCT NAME].

And like... it takes [specific task] down from [old time] to [new time]. Maybe even [time]?

Seriously. The time I've saved? I'm getting my life back.

If you're still doing this the hard way, just... try it. You'll understand."

## CUSTOMIZATION GUIDE

For each script:

1. Replace [PRODUCT NAME] with your client's actual product
2. Replace [time/number] with your authentic experience
3. Replace [benefit] with how YOU actually benefited
4. Use casual language (don't sound like an ad)
5. Show emotion (skepticism → realization → recommendation)

---

## FILMING TIPS FOR EACH

1. Casual setting (bedroom, home office, coffee shop)
2. Good natural lighting (window light is best)
3. Phone camera is fine (vertical for TikTok/Reels)
4. Be authentic (slight imperfections are MORE credible)
5. Look at camera (talking to a friend, not audience)
6. Edit lightly (jump cuts are okay, TikTok style)
7. Keep energy consistent (this is YOU, not an actor)

---

## CLIENT DELIVERY

After you film, send your client:
- Raw video files (MP4, 1080p minimum)
- File naming: [Client Name] - [Product Name] - [Version].mp4
- Quick note: "Here are 3 takes of each script. Use whichever feels most authentic."

They'll edit and post themselves, OR ask you for revisions.
