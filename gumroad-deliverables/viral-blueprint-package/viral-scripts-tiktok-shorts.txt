## HOW TO USE THESE SCRIPTS

1. Pick one
2. Replace [bracketed] sections with your real examples
3. Record vertical video (phone, natural lighting)
4. Use trending audio (Blotato shows current sounds)
5. Post to TikTok/YouTube Shorts/Reels
6. Track which hooks get highest completion rate
7. Double down on what works
8. Repeat cycle

---

## OPTIMIZATION NOTES

- Hook in first 2 seconds (non-negotiable)
- Emotional arc: Curiosity → insight → feeling
- Call-to-action: Comment, like, follow, or link
- Consistency: Post 1-2/day for algorithm favor
- Authenticity: Slight imperfections = more credible
