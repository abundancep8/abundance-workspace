## CONTENT CREATION PROMPTS

### YouTube Script Generation
1. "Write a 60-second YouTube Shorts script about [topic]. Include hook, problem, solution, and CTA. Target audience: [audience]. Tone: [tone]"

2. "Create a 90-second YouTube video script that teaches [topic] in a simple way. Include 3 key insights and 1 actionable takeaway. Start with a hook that makes people stop scrolling."

3. "Generate 5 different hooks for a video about [topic]. Make them curious, surprising, or emotionally resonant. Each hook should be 1-2 sentences max."

### Email Copy
4. "Write a subject line for an email about [topic]. Make it curiosity-driven, not clickbaity. The goal is [goal]."

5. "Write a 3-email sequence for [audience] who just signed up. Email 1: Welcome + value. Email 2: Problem identification. Email 3: Solution + offer. Each should be 150-200 words."

6. "Create an email that converts readers into customers for [product]. Include pain point, solution, proof, and CTA. Use social proof (testimonials/numbers) to build trust."

### Social Media
7. "Write 3 different Twitter/X posts about [topic] that are designed to go viral. Make them witty, insightful, or controversial (but not offensive). Include hashtags that actually work."

8. "Create a 5-tweet thread about [topic]. Start with a hook that makes people want to read all 5. Build tension and end with a CTA."

9. "Write a LinkedIn post about [topic] for [audience]. Include a personal story, lesson learned, and call-to-action. Keep it professional but authentic."

### Landing Pages
10. "Write headline + subheadline for a landing page selling [product] to [audience]. The headline should create curiosity or promise clear benefit."

11. "Write the 'above the fold' section of a landing page for [product]. Include: benefit-focused headline, subheadline, customer testimonial, CTA button text."

12. "Create a FAQ section for a landing page about [product]. Include 5 common questions and answers that address objections and build trust."

## BUSINESS AUTOMATION PROMPTS

### Product Development
13. "Help me create a digital product idea for [audience] who want [outcome]. Suggest 3 product formats (course, template, template, playbook, etc.) with pros/cons of each."

14. "I want to sell [type of product]. Create a pricing strategy with 3 tiers (basic/pro/premium). Explain the value add at each tier and estimated customer distribution."

15. "Generate a product launch sequence - email subjects and content for 5 emails that announce [product], build hype, and drive sales."

### Audience Research
16. "I'm targeting [audience]. Create 5 customer avatars with demographics, pain points, aspirations, and messaging that resonates with them."

17. "What are the top 10 questions [audience] asks about [topic]? Rank by search volume and suggest content angles for each."

18. "Create a customer ICP (Ideal Customer Profile) for [product]. Include: job title, salary, pain points, buying triggers, decision makers, etc."

### Sales & Negotiation
19. "I'm pitching [product] to [type of customer]. Draft a 2-minute pitch that leads with benefit (not features). Include objection handling."

20. "Help me negotiate a deal for [service/product]. Create 3 ask-first positions (what I want), 3 fallback positions, and my walk-away price."

## PRODUCTIVITY & WORKFLOW PROMPTS

### System Building
21. "I spend 10 hours/week on [task]. Help me automate it with [tool]. Create a step-by-step workflow including: current process, AI solution, integration points, ROI calculation."

22. "Create an ideal daily schedule for a content creator/business owner. Include: content creation, strategic work, admin, breaks, sleep. Assume 8 hours available per day."

23. "I want to delegate [task] to an AI/tool. Write a detailed prompt that an AI could execute well. Include context, constraints, and desired output format."

### Learning & Skill Building
24. "Create a 30-day learning plan for someone who wants to master [skill]. Include: daily lessons, practice exercises, milestones, resources, and expected proficiency level."

25. "Explain [complex topic] like I'm 12 years old. Use analogies and simple examples. Then escalate to a professional-level explanation."

## BONUS: PROMPT ENGINEERING TIPS

- Be specific: "Write a 500-word blog post about social media marketing for solopreneurs" is better than "Write about marketing"
- Include context: "I sell to busy entrepreneurs aged 30-50 who want to scale without hiring"
- Ask for format: "Create a bulleted list with 5 tips, each with a 1-sentence explanation"
- Iterate: If output isn't perfect, ask: "Make it more [tone], add [element], remove [element]"
