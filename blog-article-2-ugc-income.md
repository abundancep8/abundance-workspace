## What Makes a Good UGC Video

**The 4-Part Formula:**

1. **Problem (5 sec):** "I spend 2 hours daily on [task]"
2. **Solution (10 sec):** "Then I found [product]"
3. **Proof (10 sec):** "Now I spend 10 minutes. Same result."
4. **CTA (5 sec):** "Honestly, game-changer."

**Total length:** 30 seconds (SaaS prefers short, authentic, fast-paced)

**Tone:** Conversational (like talking to a friend, not an actor)

**Lighting:** Natural (natural light beats $5K lighting)

**Camera:** Phone works fine (iPhone 12+ totally sufficient)

## Common Mistakes (Don't Make These)

❌ **Over-producing:** Professional lighting + makeup + perfect script = looks fake
✅ **Do:** Natural light, casual clothes, conversational tone

❌ **Being salesy:** "This app is AMAZING!" = fails
✅ **Do:** "I was skeptical. Then I used it. Here's what surprised me."

❌ **Long videos:** 2-3 minutes = boring
✅ **Do:** 30-60 seconds max

❌ **Acting:** Trying to be perfect = inauthentic
✅ **Do:** Be yourself, show personality, slight imperfections = more credible

❌ **Ignoring the brief:** "I'll make a video how I want"
✅ **Do:** Follow the client's brief exactly (they know what converts)
