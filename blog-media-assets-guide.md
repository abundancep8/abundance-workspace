## SEO Image Optimization

**File naming:**
- ❌ "image1.jpg"
- ✅ "ai-business-dashboard-revenue-4200.jpg"

**Alt text (always include):**
- Should describe image + include relevant keywords
- Example: "YouTube Shorts dashboard showing 2,000 subscribers and monthly revenue growth"

**Image compression:**
- Use TinyPNG or Squoosh to compress before uploading
- Keep files under 500KB for fast page load

**Image dimensions:**
- Hero: 1200x630px (optimal for social sharing)
- Content: 800-1000px wide (readable on mobile)
- Icons/buttons: 200-400px

## Video Production Notes

**Tools needed (free):**
- CapCut (free video editor)
- OBS Studio (free screen recording)
- Canva (some free features)

**Quality standards:**
- Minimum: 1080p (1920x1080)
- Audio: Clear, no background noise (use quiet room)
- Pacing: Cuts every 2-3 seconds (keeps engagement)
- Length: 2-5 minutes per embedded video (keeps readers engaged)

**Hosting:**
- Embed from YouTube (free, easy)
- Or: Vimeo (nicer player)
- Or: Self-hosted on Vercel (technical, but own the data)
