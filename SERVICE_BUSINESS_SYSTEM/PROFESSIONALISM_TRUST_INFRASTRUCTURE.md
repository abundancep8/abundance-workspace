## Minimum Viable Professional Stack (MVP)

### Tier 1: ABSOLUTE MUST-HAVE (This Week)

#### 1. Business Registration (Legal)
- **Form LLC** (Delaware or your state)
  - Cost: $100-300
  - Time: 30 minutes online
  - Why: Can't legally contract without it
  - Name: Something professional (e.g., "Abundance Healthcare Automation" or your actual company name)

- **Get EIN** (Employer Identification Number)
  - Cost: Free
  - Time: 5 minutes (IRS website)
  - Why: Required for business banking + contracts

- **Business bank account**
  - Cost: Free (most banks)
  - Why: Separate personal from business money (looks professional, legal requirement)

#### 2. Professional Email
- **Domain email** (NOT Gmail)
  - Setup: buy-domain.com, use email forwarding
  - Cost: $10-15/year for domain
  - Why: "john@abundance-automation.com" looks professional; "john@gmail.com" looks like spam
  - Example: abundance-automation.com → john@abundance-automation.com

#### 3. Website (Simple but Effective)
- **Landing page** (single page, not 10 pages)
  - Tool: Webflow, Wix, or WordPress
  - Cost: $15-50/month
  - Time: 4 hours to build
  - Must-have sections:
    1. Hero (headline: "Scheduling Automation for Medical Practices")
    2. Problem (offices waste 30+ hours/week on scheduling)
    3. Solution (our system, ROI calculator)
    4. Case study (1-2 testimonials or metrics)
    5. Pricing ($12k upfront, $500-1.5k/month)
    6. CTA (book demo button)
    7. Contact info + legal stuff

#### 4. Stripe Account (Payment Processing)
- **Setup Stripe**
  - Cost: Free (2.9% + $0.30 per transaction)
  - Time: 30 minutes
  - Why: Medical offices expect Stripe (safe, trusted)
  - What it does: Collects $12k payment securely

- **Bank account linked**
  - Stripe deposits to your business bank account
  - Takes 1-2 business days

#### 5. Legal Documents
- **Terms of Service** (1 page minimum)
  - Template: TermsFeed or Termly
  - Cost: Free-$50
  - Why: Protects you, shows you're legit

- **Privacy Policy**
  - Template: Termly or iubenda
  - Cost: Free-$50
  - Why: Required by law (HIPAA if handling patient data)

## The Payment Flow (Stripe)

### How it works:
1. **Client clicks "Book Demo"**
2. **Calendly opens** → They book 15-min call
3. **You have call** → They love it
4. **Email sent:** "Ready to get started? Here's the invoice..."
5. **Link to Stripe checkout** → Client pays $12k
6. **Payment confirmed** → Deployment begins

**Alternatively:**
- Send Stripe invoice directly after demo
- Client clicks link, enters card info
- Payment processes in Stripe
- You get notified immediately
- Funds land in business bank account next business day

**Looks professional:** Medical offices expect this flow
