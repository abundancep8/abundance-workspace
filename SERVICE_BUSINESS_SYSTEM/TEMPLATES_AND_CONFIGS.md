## Sales Call Script

```
OPENING (2 min)
"Thanks for making time today! I'll keep this direct. We help 
medical practices like yours automate the reception side — 
specifically handling inbound calls so your team can focus on 
patient care instead of paperwork. Sound interesting?"

DISCOVERY (5-7 min)
Ask 1: "Walk me through a typical day — how many calls come in?"
Ask 2: "What happens when your team gets overwhelmed?"
Ask 3: "What's the impact on your business?"
Ask 4: "How many people are currently handling calls?"

DEMO (5-7 min)
"Here's a real call from a clinic like yours. Our AI answers, 
books an appointment, and transfers if needed. 
[PLAY: 60-second demo]
Questions so far?"

OBJECTION HANDLING (3-5 min)
Objection 1: "Will patients be frustrated with AI?"
Response: "Patients actually prefer it — no on-hold music, 
instant response, instant booking. Plus, for complex issues, 
it transfers to your team immediately."

Objection 2: "What if it makes a mistake?"
Response: "It won't — it's trained on your specific workflow. 
We also have a 30-day optimization period to fine-tune based 
on your real calls."

Objection 3: "How much is this?"
Response: "$12,000 upfront setup. Saves you $15-20k the first 
year. ROI is typically 2-3 months."

CLOSE (2-3 min)
Soft Close: "Based on what you've told me, this is a perfect fit. 
The question is 'when do you want to get started?' Next week 
or the week after?"

If NO: "How about a 7-day pilot? No payment upfront. If you're 
not seeing results after a week, we part as friends."

---

AFTER CALL
├─ Send proposal within 30 min
├─ Schedule 48-hour follow-up
└─ Log call details to memory
```
