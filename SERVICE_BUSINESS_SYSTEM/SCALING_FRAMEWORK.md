## Month 1: Solo (You + Me)
```
Activities per week:
├─ 50 leads generated (automated)
├─ 40+ outreach messages sent (automated)
├─ 5-8 demos scheduled (you take calls)
├─ 2-3 proposals sent (automated)
```

## Month 2: Hire Sales BDR

**Decision point:** Revenue reached $48k+ (proof of concept)

**What to hire:**
- 1 Business Development Rep (BDR)
- Part-time ($2k/month)
- Responsibilities:
  - Take 50% of demo calls
  - Qualify leads (yes/no)
  - Schedule proposals

**Impact:**
- Your time drops to 20 min/day
- Demo capacity doubles
- Close rate improves (more focus)
- Revenue scales 1.5x

**New metrics:**
```
Activities per week:
├─ 100+ leads (increased outreach budget)
├─ 80+ outreach messages
├─ 12-16 demos (you + BDR)
├─ 4-6 proposals
├─ 3-4 deals ($12k each)

Revenue: $36-48k/week
Profit: ~$30-40k/week
Your time: 20 min/day
```

## When to Scale Each Layer

**Scale hiring when:**
- Your time at capacity (45+ min/day)
- Demo conversion suffers (drop <35%)
- Support backlog grows (>2 hour response time)

**Scale features when:**
- Client satisfaction drops (<9/10)
- Churn increases (>10% monthly)
- Upsell adoption plateaus (<40%)

**Scale verticals when:**
- Current vertical fully penetrated (>5 competitors won)
- ROI proven (>60% of deals close)
- Replicable playbook documented

## Risk Management

### Hiring Risk
```
Mitigation:
├─ Contract-to-hire (30-day trial)
├─ Performance-based bonuses
├─ Clear KPIs (BDR: 8+ demos/week)
└─ Backup plan (freelancer list ready)
```

### Churn Risk
```
Mitigation:
├─ Monthly success reviews (automated)
├─ Proactive outreach at usage drops
├─ Upsell before they quit
├─ Monthly value reinforcement
└─ Target: <5% monthly churn
```

### Market Risk
```
Mitigation:
├─ Multiple verticals (not just medical)
├─ Multiple ICP profiles (small/medium/large)
├─ Multiple price points ($8k/$12k/$15k)
└─ Multiple sale channels (cold + inbound + referral)
```
