# LinkedIn Outreach Automation Sequence
**Target:** Medical practice owners, clinic managers, small B2B businesses  
**Goal:** Book 5-8 demos/week  
**Automation Level:** Fully autonomous with human approval option

---

## Lead Profile

**Ideal Target:**
- Title: Practice Owner, Practice Manager, Operations Manager, Clinic Director
- Industry: Medical, Dental, Veterinary, Physical Therapy, Legal, Accounting
- Company size: 2-20 employees
- Pain point: Handling high call volume, appointment scheduling bottleneck

## Automation Sequence (5 touchpoints over 14 days)

### Touch 1: Connection Request (Day 1)
**Platform:** LinkedIn  
**Type:** Connection request with personalized message  
**Message Template:**

```
Hi [First Name],

I work with [similar clinic/business] in your area helping them handle the 
administrative side more efficiently. Saw you're managing [clinic name] and 
thought you might find this useful.

Would you be open to a quick 15-min chat?

[Your Name]
```

**Automation:** Auto-send to 5 qualified leads/day (Monday-Friday, 9 AM local time)  
**Success metric:** 30-40% acceptance rate

---

### Touch 2: Follow-up Message (Day 3)
**Platform:** LinkedIn (if connected) or Email (if available)  
**Type:** Value-add message  
**Message Template:**

```
Hey [First Name],

No worries if you missed my last message. I've been working with clinics 
like yours that say their biggest bottleneck is handling call volume during 
peak hours.

We recently helped [clinic name] cut their reception workload by 60% using 
AI automation. Thought it might be worth a conversation.

Free 15-min call if you're curious? I can walk you through how it works.

[Your Name]
```

**Automation:** Auto-send if no response to Touch 1  
**Success metric:** 20-30% response rate

---

### Touch 3: Social Proof Demo (Day 5)
**Platform:** Email + LinkedIn  
**Type:** Case study + demo video  
**Content:**

```
Subject: [Clinic Name] Case Study - How They Handle 80% More Calls

Hi [First Name],

Quick follow-up. Attached is a short case study of how [similar clinic] 
automated their appointment booking with AI.

[INCLUDE: 30-second video demo of AI handling a real call]

They went from 3 staff handling calls to 1 person managing follow-ups.

Interested in a quick walkthrough? Link below:
[Calendly scheduling link]

[Your Name]
```

**Automation:** Auto-send email with tracked opens  
**Success metric:** 15-25% demo scheduled from this touch

---

### Touch 4: Time-Sensitive CTA (Day 7)
**Platform:** LinkedIn + Email  
**Type:** Limited-time offer  
**Message Template:**

```
Hey [First Name],

We're currently running a pilot program where we're setting up 5 practices 
in your area with AI automation at a discounted rate.

We have 2 spots left, so wanted to reach out directly.

Are you free for a 15-min call this week? You can book here: [Calendly]

If not now, totally understand. Just wanted to make sure you had the option.

[Your Name]
```

**Automation:** Only send if previous touches had low engagement  
**Success metric:** Urgency typically increases response by 25%

---

### Touch 5: Final Breakup Message (Day 14)
**Platform:** LinkedIn  
**Type:** Final attempt + unsubscribe option  
**Message Template:**

```
Hey [First Name],

Last attempt from my side. I know you're busy, and if this isn't relevant 
to what you're working on, that's totally cool.

But if you ever want to chat about handling call volume better, I'm here.

Take care,
[Your Name]

P.S. Not interested? No problem, I won't reach out again.
```

**Automation:** Auto-send if no response after Touch 4  
**Success metric:** Final 5-10% conversion (often from people who were "waiting to see")

## Automation Rules

**Scoring System:**
- Connection accepted: +10 points
- Message opened: +5 points
- Link clicked: +15 points
- Video watched (>50%): +20 points
- Calendar link clicked: +30 points
- Demo scheduled: MOVE TO SALES (automatic)

**Auto-Actions:**
- Score >30: Move to "High Priority" pipeline
- Score 15-30: Keep in sequence
- Score <15 after 14 days: Move to "Nurture" list (re-engage in 30 days)
- Demo scheduled: Send prep email + calendar reminder

**Rules-Based Exceptions:**
- If reply detected: Pause automation, flag for human review
- If "not interested" phrase detected: Remove from list, add to "Do Not Contact"
- If out-of-office reply: Snooze 2 weeks, auto-resume
