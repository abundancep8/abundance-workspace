# Automated Sales Call System
**Goal:** Close 3-4 deals/week ($36k-48k revenue) with minimal human time  
**Format:** 15-30 minute discovery calls → Demo → Proposal → Close  
**Automation:** Fully scripted with human approval gates

## Sales Call Script (15-30 min)

### **Opening (2 min)**
```
Thanks for making time today! 

I know you're busy, so I'll keep this direct.

We help medical practices like yours automate the reception side — 
specifically handling inbound calls so your team can focus on 
patient care instead of paperwork.

Sound interesting?
```

**Pause for response.** (Show confidence, not desperation)

---

### **Discovery (5-7 min)**

**Ask 1:** "Walk me through a typical day — how many calls come in?"
- *Listen for:* Call volume, peak hours, what's frustrating about it

**Ask 2:** "What happens when your team gets overwhelmed?"
- *Listen for:* Dropped calls, long hold times, rescheduled callbacks

**Ask 3:** "What's the impact on your business?"
- *Listen for:* Missed revenue, patient dissatisfaction, staff burnout

**Ask 4:** "How many people are currently handling calls?"
- *Listen for:* Cost justification (staff salary vs. $12k)

**Transition:** "Based on what you're telling me, I think we can help. 
Let me show you how."

---

### **Demo (5-7 min)**

**Setup:** Screen share (or pre-recorded if timezone issue)

```
What you're about to see is a real call from a clinic like yours. 
Our AI answers, books an appointment, and transfers if needed.

[PLAY: 60-second demo video of AI handling call]

Here's what happened:
- AI answered on ring 1 (your prospect says "hello")
- AI identified the need (appointment request)
- AI booked the appointment in the calendar
- AI sent confirmation text to patient

Meanwhile, your team is handling patients in the room. Zero disruption.
```

**Ask:** "Does this look like something that would help your practice?"

---

### **Objection Handling (3-5 min)**

**Objection 1: "Will patients be frustrated talking to AI?"**
```
Great question. Patients actually prefer it — no on-hold music, 
instant response, instant booking. 

[STAT: 82% of patients prefer AI for routine calls]

Plus, for complex issues, it transfers to your team immediately.
```

**Objection 2: "What if it makes a mistake?"**
```
It won't — it's trained on your specific workflow. And even if something 
unusual comes up, it transfers to your team instantly.

We also have a 30-day optimization period where we fine-tune it based 
on your real calls.
```

**Objection 3: "How much is this?"**
```
$12,000 upfront setup fee. Includes deployment, training, 90 days support.

Average practice saves $15-20k the first year in staff time alone. 

ROI is typically 2-3 months. Does that feel reasonable?
```

---

### **Close (2-3 min)**

**Soft Close #1:**
```
Based on what you've told me, this is a perfect fit. 

The question isn't really "should we do this?" but "when do you 
want to get started?"

We can have this live for your team in 14 days. Would you rather 
start next week or the week after?
```

**If YES:** Move to **Close Script** below  
**If NO:** Go to **Objection #3** or **Soft Close #2**

**Soft Close #2:**
```
I totally get the hesitation. This is new technology.

How about we do a 7-day pilot? You don't pay anything upfront. 
If you're not seeing results after a week, we part as friends.

Fair?
```

**If still NO:** 
```
No problem at all. Sometimes the timing isn't right.

Can I check back in 30 days? Things might be different then.

In the meantime, here's our case study [link]. Feel free to 
read through when you get a chance.

Thanks for your time!
```

## Close Script (Once they say YES)

```
Awesome! Here's how this works:

STEP 1 (Tomorrow): You sign the agreement + provide clinic info
STEP 2 (This week): We audit your current setup (calls, calendar, etc.)
STEP 3 (Week 2): We deploy + train your team
STEP 4 (Week 3): You go live
STEP 5 (Weeks 4-12): 90-day support + optimization

Total time from today to live: 14 days.

I'm going to send you a proposal today. Take a look, sign, and send 
it back. Once I have it, we're officially starting.

Sound good?
```

**Automation:** Send proposal 30 min after call ends (auto-generated)

## Key Rules

✅ **Always:**
- Ask discovery questions (don't pitch first)
- Show proof before asking for money
- Ask for the sale directly
- Offer a guarantee or trial
- Send proposal same day

❌ **Never:**
- Negotiate below $12,000 upfront
- Skip the demo
- Promise results in <30 days
- Work with practices that don't have calendar systems
- Take the call without prep email sent
