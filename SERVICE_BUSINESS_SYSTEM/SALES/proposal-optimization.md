## Objection Pre-Emption

Address concerns before they ask:

```
"Investment is expensive for our budget"
Pre-empted in proposal:
"Investment: $12,000 (typically pays for itself in 2 weeks)"

"Will patients be frustrated with AI?"
Pre-empted in proposal:
"Patient satisfaction increases 15-20% with instant responses"

"What if it doesn't work for us?"
Pre-empted in proposal:
"90-day guarantee: Full refund if not satisfied"

"How long to implement?"
Pre-empted in proposal:
"Go-live in 14 days. Minimal disruption."

Result: Removes objections before call, increases close rate
```
